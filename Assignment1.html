The Library

Located on the top floor of the Campus Center, the NYU Abu Dhabi Library provides spaces for engagement between faculty and students, complemented by quiet areas for concentration and contemplation. Comfortable reading areas and study rooms create a productive atmosphere for research.

The library includes more than 91,000 physical books, over 7,400 DVDs, over 416,000 e-journals, over 1,400 databases, and nearly 5 million ebooks.

Beyond the Library

Even though the library's book collection is huge, it is not the only book collection on campus.

You can find book collections at:

  • The Interactive Media Lab at C3
  • The Design Studio in C3
  • The Bookstore in A6
  • The Unix Lab in C2
  • The Writing Center in C2
  • The Engineering Design Studio in A5
  • Professors' Offices
  • Your Friends' Dorms
  • And many more
